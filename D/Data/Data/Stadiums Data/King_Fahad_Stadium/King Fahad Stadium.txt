(General Stadium Information)
- King Fahad Sports City Stadium is one of the most iconic venues in the region.
- It is recognizable for its distinctive fabric roof inspired by traditional tents.
- It is recognizable for its elegant podium.
- This is a multi-purpose stadium.
- It is the current home of the Saudi Arabian national football team.
- It is used to host Saudi Premier League (SPL) games.
- It has an average attendance of approximately 23,000 for all major events.
- It has hosted international sports tournaments.
- It has hosted large-scale entertainment productions such as concerts.
- It has hosted large-scale entertainment productions such as motorsports.
- It will be used for the AFC Asian Cup in 2027.
- The stadium currently seats more than 58,000 spectators.
- The design of the refurbishment is guided by the vision of an all-encompassing football city.
- It will have an expanded capacity of over 70,000.
- The King Fahad Sports City Stadium is a prominent sports venue.
- It integrates with the wider city community.
- The stadium’s location is near several attractions such as the Wadi As-Sulai.
- It is near the planned Sports Boulevard which will provide access to green spaces and natural surroundings.
- After the FIFA World Cup 2034™, King Fahad Sports City stadium will serve as a multi-purpose sports and entertainment venue.
