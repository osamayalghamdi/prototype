(General Stadium Information)

Q: When was King Abdullah Sports City Stadium built?
A: It was built in 2014.   

Q: How does King Abdullah Stadium rank in size among stadiums in the country?
A: It is currently the country’s second largest stadium.   

Q: How many fans can the stadium hold?
A: It has a capacity to seat more than 57,000 spectators.   

Q: Which football clubs call King Abdullah Stadium home?
A: It is the home to the Al Ittihad football club and the Al Ahli football club.   

Q: What's the typical attendance for big events at the stadium?
A: It has an average attendance of approximately 46,000 for major events.   

Q: Will King Abdullah Stadium be used for any upcoming tournaments?
A: Yes, it will also be used for the AFC Asian Cup in 2027.   

Q: Was the stadium used for the FIFA Club World Cup Saudi Arabia 2023?
A: Yes, this was a key venue during the FIFA Club World Cup Saudi Arabia 2023™. The FIFA Club World Cup Saudi Arabia 2023™ had an average attendance of 50,000 at this venue.   

Q: What is the stadium's setting like?
A: The stadium is set among extensive sports and recreational facilities.   

Q: Does the stadium have a nickname?
A: Yes, the stadium is also known as 'The Shining Jewel'.   

Q: Why is it called 'The Shining Jewel'?
A: Its nickname comes from its distinctive geometric architecture.   

Q: Where is King Abdullah Stadium located?
A: The stadium is located to the north of central Jeddah. It is close to the airport.   

Q: What other facilities are part of the sports precinct?
A: Beyond the main arena, the precinct has a variety of sports facilities. These facilities include outdoor football training pitches, an outdoor athletics track, tennis courts, and a large multi-purpose sports hall. Within the sports city, there are additional facilities for the community, referees, players building, and theater.   





(Stadium Owner and Operator)

Q: Who owns King Abdullah Stadium?
A: The stadium owner is the Ministry of Sport.   

Q: Who operates King Abdullah Stadium?
A: The stadium operator is the Ministry of Sport.   

Q: What is the future primary use of the stadium?
A: The future primary legacy use is KSA national team stadium.   





(Stadium Capacity)

Q: What is the gross and net capacity of King Abdullah Stadium?
A: The gross capacity is 58,432, and the net capacity is 54,127.   

Q: How many hospitality seats are available?
A: There are 4,416 hospitality seats.   

Q: How many sky box seats are there?
A: There are 1,462 sky box seats (in 54 boxes).   

Q: How many media tribune seats are there?
A: There are 1,584 media tribune seats.   

Q: How many VVIP and VIP seats are available?
A: There are 163 VVIP seats and 1,032 VIP seats.   





(Pitch)
Q: What are the dimensions of the field of play and the pitch area?
A: The field of play dimensions are 105m × 68m, and the pitch area dimensions are 125m × 85m.   

Q: What type of pitch does the stadium have?
A: It has a FIFA World Cup™ type of pitch: Hybrid.   





(Technical Installations)

Q: What is the stadium's orientation?
A: The stadium orientation is -40°.   

Q: Is the seating area covered?
A: Yes, all seats are covered.   

Q: What is the floodlight lux level?
A: The floodlight lux (horizontal) is 2,500 lux.   

Q: How many video screens are inside the stadium?
A: There are 2 video screens.   





(Accessibility)
